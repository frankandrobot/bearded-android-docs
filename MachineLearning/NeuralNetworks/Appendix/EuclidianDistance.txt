====== EuclidianDistance ======
Created Wednesday 20 November 2013

The **Euclidian distance** (or **Euclidian norm**) of two vectors `bb x` and `bb y` is
 
`||bb x - bb y|| = sqrt((bb x - bb y) * (bb x - bb y)) = sqrt( (x_i - y_i)^2 + cdots + (x_n - y_n)^2 )`
