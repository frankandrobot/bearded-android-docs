====== ChainRule ======
Created Monday 18 November 2013

* http://archives.math.utk.edu/visual.calculus/2/chain_rule.4/

Let F be the composition of two differentiable functions f and g; `F(x) = f(g(x))`. 
Then F is differentiable and

$$F'(x) = f'(g(x)) g'(x)$$
